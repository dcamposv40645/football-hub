Football Hub – Privacy Policy

Effective: May 2025

Football Hub does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Local storage

The only data stored is your selected team preference, saved locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your browser.

External requests

The extension fetches publicly available football data from ESPN (site.api.espn.com) and Club Elo (api.clubelo.com). No personally identifiable information is included in these requests.

No tracking

Football Hub contains no analytics, no advertising, no tracking scripts, and no third-party SDKs.
